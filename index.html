Why play tennis with us?

To keep fit

Tennis is a good sport for maintaining health, fitness and agility, as well as being a fun way to exercise.

To make friends

There are plenty of opportunities to play tennis and make friends, with several club social events per year.

To compete!

Tournaments, interclub and county matches run all summer, plus year round friendly matches.
